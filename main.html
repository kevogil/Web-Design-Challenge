In this project, we deep dive into the ways the weather changes as we get closer to the equator.

To tackle this, we utilize the OpenWeatherMap API to gather data across 550 cities all over the world.

Through Pandas and Matplotlib, we visualize our dataset to plot various measurements of weather from temperature, humidity, wind speeds, and cloudiness levels.

Let's see what we found out!
