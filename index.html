Summary

In this project, we deep dive into the ways the weather changes as we get closer to the equator.

To tackle this, we utilize the OpenWeatherMap API to gather data across 546 cities all over the world.

Through Pandas and Matplotlib, we visualize our dataset to plot various measurements of weather from temperature, humidity, wind speeds, and cloudiness levels.

We'll walk you through the visualizations and explain any correlations, or lack of correlation, found between the various measurements.

Let's see what we can find out!
